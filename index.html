Accommodation Details

The Space

The Loft is a self-contained bedroom/living room on the first floor of our double garage, with own kitchen and bathroom on the ground floor. Accessed by its own front door.

Keep cosy in the cooler months with a fire in the woodburner. Newly installed gas central heating and double glazing ensure year-round comfort.

There are various heat sources including the woodburner upstairs & newly installed gas central heating. There is also an electric fire and an electric blanket on the bed. The bathroom has an electric heated towel rail for the summer months and one that runs on the hot water system.

Views & Garden

The Loft does not have use of garden or outside space, however there are views to the rear of our wooded garden and beautiful countryside beyond.
